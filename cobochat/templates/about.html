About CoboChat

CoboChat is a project created by Cohen with the intention to host a care-free environment to post thoughts, expressions, opinions, and daily feelings.

This program is extremely restricted, with accounts only given to those that have a relationship with Cohen. This is simply to maintain the security and anonymity of the users.

The project was made from scratch by Cohen, with networking help from Foz and motivation from John.
